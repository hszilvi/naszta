Data Management Policy

1. Introduction

The purpose of this Data Management Policy is to inform visitors of naszta.hu (hereinafter: the Website) about the processing of personal data, with particular attention to the use of Google Analytics.

Operator of the Website:
Name: Ferenc Nasztanovics
Contact: see contact info on Homepage

The Website uses only the Google Analytics service to create website statistics.

2. Legal Basis of Data Processing

The use of Google Analytics is based on Article 6(1)(a) of the GDPR, meaning the voluntary consent of the user, which is provided through the cookie-management interface.

Users may withdraw their consent at any time by modifying their cookie settings.

3. Purpose of Data Processing

The data collected by Google Analytics is used for the following purposes:

  • analyzing the use of the Website,
  • generating statistics on visitor traffic,
  • improving user experience,
  • identifying technical issues.

The data is not used for profiling or marketing purposes.

4. Scope of Processed Data

Google Analytics may collect the following types of data:

  • IP address (partially anonymized)
  • browser type and version
  • operating system type
  • device type
  • date and duration of the visit
  • pages viewed
  • referring website
  • click and navigation data

The Website does not directly collect personal data (such as name, email address, phone number, etc.).

5. Use of Cookies

The Website uses only the cookies required for Google Analytics to function.

Cookies used:

  • _ga – distinguishes users
  • _gid – distinguishes users for a short period
  • _gat – regulates request rate

Users may accept or reject cookies.

6. Data Processor

The Website uses the services of Google LLC (1600 Amphitheatre Parkway, Mountain View, CA 94043, USA).

Google may store data outside the EU, applying appropriate safeguards (e.g., EU–US Data Privacy Framework).

7. Data Retention Period

The retention period for data collected by Google Analytics: 14 months / or the period you choose.

Users may delete their cookies at any time in their browser.

8. User Rights

Users have the following rights:

  • right of access
  • right to rectification
  • right to erasure (“right to be forgotten”)
  • right to restriction of processing
  • right to withdraw consent
  • right to lodge a complaint with the NAIH

9. Ensuring Data Security

The operator of the Website takes all reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the data. Data processing by Google Analytics occurs through encrypted channels.

10. Modification of the Policy

The operator reserves the right to modify the Data Management Policy. Any modifications enter into force upon publication on the Website.
